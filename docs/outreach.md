# Market Validation & Outreach Report: Veterinary SaaS

## 1. Executive Summary
This document summarizes the findings from our **pre-MVP cold outreach** phase. The primary objective was to validate whether veterinary clinics in the Serbian market experience the pain points we identified and to assess the competitive landscape before committing to full-scale development.

---

## 2. Competitive Intelligence
Our outreach revealed a significant **market gap** regarding client engagement and data transparency.

### The "Universal Gap"
* **Lack of Client-Facing Software:** None of the existing competitors offer a portal for pet owners.
* **Information Silos:** Current systems are designed exclusively for internal clinic use, leaving pet owners reliant on physical papers or phone calls for history and reminders.

---

## 3. Outreach Logs & Competitor Profiles

### **ProvetSA**
* **Source:** [provetsa.in.rs](https://provetsa.in.rs/)
* **Response:** Highly defensive/rude. Directly stated: *"We wouldn't even let our clients see real data."*
* **Key Insight:** There is a significant cultural barrier regarding data transparency. Our platform can disrupt this by positioning transparency as a "trust-builder" rather than a risk.

### **IQVet**
* **Source:** [iqvet.net](https://www.iqvet.net/)
* **Response:** Clinics confirmed active use.
* **Key Insight:** * **Legacy Tech:** The system is outdated and lacks modern UI/UX.
    * **High Friction:** The price point is approximately **400 EUR**, likely a flat fee or high entry cost, making a subscription-based (SaaS) model a more attractive alternative for smaller clinics.

### **Custom MS Access Systems**
* **Nature:** Internal, custom-built databases.
* **Key Insight:** Many clinics use localized solutions that are not cloud-based. These systems offer zero scalability and no way for pet owners to interact with their pets' data remotely.

---

## 4. Strategic Implications for MVP
Based on this data, the MVP should prioritize the following:
1.  **Pet Owner Dashboard:** Focus on medical history, digital vaccination records, and automated reminders as our primary **Unique Selling Proposition (USP)**.
2.  **Modern Pricing:** Undercut the heavy upfront costs of legacy systems like IQVet with a flexible monthly subscription.
3.  **Ease of Migration:** Since many clinics use old or custom systems, providing a simple way to import existing data will be a critical success factor.
