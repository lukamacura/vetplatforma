# VetPlatforma — Marketing Plan

_Grounded in 4 real conversations with Serbian vets (Bojan, PVA PetMedica, Pet Clinic, Milovan), April 2026._

---

## 1. What the conversations actually told us

Four vet-side contacts, four very different stances. The pattern matters more than any single quote.

### Bojan (FTN student, Novi Sad)
Not a customer — a **fellow student building the same thing**. Important signal: the idea is obvious enough that others are already chasing it. Speed and distribution matter more than the concept.

> _"Naravno, pošto kolega sa fakulteta i ja radimo na ovome, trenutno pravimo neki dizajn kako bi to otprilike izgledalo…"_

**Implication:** we are not first-to-idea. We must be first-to-clinic.

### PVA PetMedica — uses **Provet**
Flatly rejects the core pet-owner-sees-chart premise.

> _"Ne vidim razlog zasto bi vlasnik imao uvid u karton niti bi ta mogucnost trebao da ima. Pacijent nije doktor."_
> _"Nema potrebe da vlasnik ima bilo kakav uvid u karton jer sve to dobija u izveštajima i takođe mu se upisuje i u pasoš."_

**Implication:** clinics already on a real PMS see owner access as a **liability, not a feature**. Don't lead with "karton u telefonu vlasnika." Lead with what's missing from Provet: the owner-facing reminder/booking layer.

### Pet Clinic (Novi Sad) — uses **custom MS Access DB**
Open, engaged, but has a **specific fear**: owners self-medicating based on Facebook groups.

> _"Sve informacije koje su bitne za lečenje, kao i terapije bi mogle biti vrlo lako zloupotrebljene… znamo da vlasnici umeju sami da sprovode terapije na osnovu iskustava drugih vlasnika. (Npr fejsbuk grupe mačkara — vlasnici prvo pišu post na grupi gde pitaju i sprovode sami terapije pre nego što će kontaktirati svog veterinara)."_

But she wants **preventive reminders, vaccination calendar, education, and a way to keep clients loyal**:

> _"Najveći akcenat vlasnicima treba da bude edukacija o preventivnim merama, vakcinaciji i slično."_
> _"Kako bih dodala kako bi se poboljšala lojalnost između klinike i klijenata…"_

**Implication:** the safe, winning owner-side feature set is **calendar + reminders + preventive education + vet-curated recommendations** — not therapy history. Position the vet as the filter between the owner and the internet.

### Milovan — uses old vet-specific software, values phone calls
Traditionalist. Phone is sacred. But notices generational split and is willing to try.

> _"Najviše obavljamo direktno telefonski ili uživo sa vlasnicima, jer se najbolje pokazalo… najviše cene direktnu komunikaciju."_
> _"Svaka ambulanta ima drugačiju klijentelu, tako nešto više odgovara mlađoj populaciji dok kod starije ne znam kakva bi reakcija bila."_
> _"Čuli smo za softvere koji su kreirani, zbog toga smo i došli do ideje da bi kreiranje aplikacije, koja bi bila pristupačna za naše ambulante i ne bi bila samo interna, već bi i korisnici imali uvid u svoj nalog."_

He already wants: owner nalog, auto-reminders for next visit (vaccinations, controls), central place for pet info, **automatic billing and statistics** on the vet side.

**Implication:** Milovan is the ideal Founding Partner persona — already convinced, just needs a finished product. Build for him.

## 2. The real insight (what changed from the initial framework)

The first draft assumed "pet's lifetime medical record in the owner's phone" was the hero. **The conversations say no.**

- Vets with a real PMS (Provet) **don't want owners seeing therapies, diagnoses, or drug dosages.** Legal and self-medication risk.
- Vets without a PMS (MS Access, old vet software) **do want an owner channel — but for prevention, reminders, and loyalty, not records.**
- All of them agree on: **reminders, calendar, vaccinations, reduction of phone load, client retention.**

**The wedge is the reminder + booking layer, owned by the vet, safe by default.** The "record" stays internal. The owner sees only what the vet explicitly publishes (next vaccine date, next control, preventive note, recommended food).

This is a **smaller promise but a more acceptable one**, and it's the one that actually closes clinics on both sides of the PMS divide.

## 3. Revised positioning

> **VetPlatforma je sloj između vaše klinike i vlasnika — automatski podsetnici, zakazivanje i edukacija, bez haosa na telefonu i bez rizika da vlasnik sam sebi "prepisuje" terapiju.**

Three things it is:
1. **Vet-controlled owner channel** — vet decides what's visible.
2. **Reminder & booking engine** — vaccinations, controls, preventive care, auto SMS/push.
3. **Loyalty layer** — owner's pet lives in _your_ clinic's app, not a Facebook group.

Three things it is **not** (explicit, on the landing page):
- Not a medical record for owners.
- Not a self-service diagnosis tool.
- Not a replacement for Provet or existing PMS — it sits next to it.

This last point disarms the PVA PetMedica objection directly.

## 4. Marketing angles, re-ranked against real quotes

### 🥇 Angle 1 — "Klijent u aplikaciji, a ne na Fejsbuk grupi" (Loyalty & Safety)
**Insight from:** Pet Clinic's Facebook-group fear + Milovan's loyalty concern.
**Positioning:** _Vaši klijenti više ne traže savet na grupama — traže ga od vas, u vašoj aplikaciji._
**Pillars:**
- Vet-curated preventive content (vaccines, parasites, nutrition)
- Owner sees only what vet approves
- "Pitaj svog veterinara" in-app channel
- Loyalty metric: % klijenata aktivnih u aplikaciji

**Channel:** Facebook vet groups (Srbija), direct message to clinic owners, 1:1 demos.
**Why first:** addresses a pain clinics **articulated themselves, unprompted**. Highest emotional resonance.

### 🥈 Angle 2 — "Automatski podsetnici, manje praznih termina" (Fill the Chair)
**Insight from:** Milovan explicitly naming _"podsećanje na predstojećoj vakcinaciji, preventivnim tretmanima"_ and Pet Clinic already having a dedicated section for this in her Access DB.
**Positioning:** _Svaka vakcina i kontrola na vreme — automatski, bez poziva iz recepcije._
**Pillars:**
- Auto vaccine/control reminders (SMS + push)
- Owner one-tap booking of the reminded appointment
- ROI math: 1 recovered control/week ≫ 49€
- Works alongside Provet / existing software

**Channel:** Landing page ROI calculator, cold outreach, vet conferences.
**Why second:** rational, measurable, easy to A/B.

### 🥉 Angle 3 — "Osnivački partner — 10 mesta, 49€ zauvek" (Scarcity / Tier 1)
**Insight from:** Bojan confirms competitors exist; Milovan is ready to test an unfinished product; Pet Clinic offered to actively collaborate on design.
**Positioning:** _Prvih 10 klinika gradi VetPlatformu sa nama. Zauvek plaćaju 49€._
**Pillars:**
- Limited seats, lifetime lock
- Co-design with founding vets (Pet Clinic already volunteered)
- Direct line to founders
- First-mover in SRB vet SaaS

**Channel:** 1:1 WhatsApp/Viber, existing warm contacts (Milovan, Pet Clinic), FTN network.
**Why third:** converts warm leads we already have. Doesn't work for cold traffic without one of the angles above.

### Angle 4 — "Kraj telefonskom haosu" (Phone relief) — **demote**
Milovan **explicitly values phone calls** as the best client channel. Leading with "end the phone chaos" will alienate traditionalist vets. Keep as a secondary message for younger urban clinics only.

### Angle 5 — "Karton koji prati ljubimca ceo život" — **drop**
PVA PetMedica killed this angle on sight. Do not lead with it. It can exist as a **vet-internal** feature ("vaš karton, čist i pretraživ"), but never as an owner-facing promise.

## 5. Segmentation — who to sell to first

| Segment | Example | PMS status | Buying signal | Angle |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A. Modern, no real PMS** | Pet Clinic, Milovan | MS Access / old vet software | Already wants owner app | 1 + 3 |
| **B. Traditional, phone-first** | Milovan's older peers | Old vet software | Needs proof younger clients demand it | 2 |
| **C. Already on Provet / ezyVet** | PVA PetMedica | Real PMS | Objects to owner access | Skip for now — revisit as _companion layer_ in v2 |

**Go-to-market order:** A → B → C. Segment C is not a Tier 1 buyer. Don't waste the 10 founding seats on them.

## 6. Messaging dos and don'ts (from actual quotes)

**Do say:**
- "Vlasnik vidi samo ono što Vi odobrite."
- "Podsetnici na vakcine i kontrole — automatski."
- "Sloj između Vaše klinike i vlasnika, ne zamena za postojeći softver."
- "Klijenti se vraćaju jer ih Vaša klinika živi u njihovom telefonu."
- "Prvih 10 klinika, zauvek 49€."

**Do not say:**
- "Ljubimčev karton u telefonu vlasnika." _(PVA PetMedica rejected this verbatim.)_
- "Zamenite Vaš postojeći softver." _(Nobody wants a migration.)_
- "Više nema telefonskih poziva." _(Milovan considers the phone a feature.)_
- "Vlasnik može da prati terapiju." _(Pet Clinic's self-medication fear.)_

## 7. Landing page changes (index.html, tier2_offer.html)

Concrete edits to ground the new positioning:

1. **Hero subheadline** → shift from any "karton / record" language to: _"Automatski podsetnici, zakazivanje i edukacija vlasnika — bez haosa na telefonu."_
2. **New section: "Šta vlasnik NE vidi"** — explicit bullet list (terapije, doze, dijagnoze). This single section converts skeptical segment-C vets and reassures segment-A vets who share Pet Clinic's fear.
3. **Social proof placeholder** → quote from Pet Clinic on education-first framing (with permission) or Milovan on reminder value.
4. **"Radi uz Vaš postojeći softver"** — a one-liner on the page. Removes the #1 cold objection.
5. **ROI block** — keep, but reframe around _recovered vaccinations/controls_ rather than generic "no-shows," since that's the language vets used.

## 8. 30-day action plan

1. **Week 1** — Rewrite `index.html` hero + add "Šta vlasnik ne vidi" section. Get Pet Clinic and Milovan on a call together (Pet Clinic already offered: _"još bolje da se nađemo nas troje"_). Use the call to co-design the reminder flow.
2. **Week 2** — Ship a clickable prototype of the reminder + owner-view flow. Send to both as founding partners under the 49€ lock.
3. **Week 3** — Post the "Klijent u aplikaciji, a ne na grupi" angle into 2–3 Serbian vet Facebook groups as a discussion post (not an ad) to validate Angle 1 resonance at scale.
4. **Week 4** — Cold outreach to 20 Novi Sad / Belgrade clinics using Angle 1 as the opener and Angle 2 as the proof. Goal: 3 demos, 1 signed founding partner beyond the existing two.

---

## 9. Biggest risk surfaced by the conversations

**Bojan is building the same thing.** He's a student at FTN Novi Sad, has a co-founder, and is in the same user-research phase we are. He contacted the same vets. Assume 3–5 other teams are doing the same. The moat is not the idea — it's **locking the first 10 clinics onto a 49€-forever contract before anyone else ships**. Every week without a signed founding partner is a week that window closes.

**This is the reason Angle 3 (founder scarcity) must run in parallel with Angle 1**, not after it.
